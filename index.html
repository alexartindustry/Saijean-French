WHY

Choose Me?

I believe every student learns differently. Which is why I only provide one on one private lessons. I design a lesson plan for each student of mine. Every one learns at a different pace and asks different kinds of questions. It is imperative to me that the student is having fun while learning important basic foundations and fundamentals of playing an instrument. And applying what they learn to create their own music and another way to express themselves. Additionally I provide monthly plans for parents that would like to buy lessons at one time. With that being said I do offer lessons that can be purchased individually, that are refundable. Learning an instrument is a journey. One that you will love!

What's your

teaching style?

Every lesson plan is customized to each student. Working with all students of all ages I've come to the realization that there is no one way of teaching, no handbook full of lesson plans that tells you exactly how to teach a student. Which can be frustrating at times and also rewarding. Creating a lesson that only revolves around the student and how they learn is one of the most important things to me. This can be imperative for a young student that is first encountering how to learn an instrument. Learning an instrument let alone a new instrument can be easily discouraging for a student. Incorporating a teaching style that makes things exciting, and rewarding is my goal.

What is your typical process

for working with a new customer?

Typically with a new student I will have them contact me and we will go over rates and goals with guitar. I will ask if the student has an instrument to start on and if interested I can recommend the best beginner instrument for the student. After that I will set up a time and day to meet up with potential student so both the parent and student can meet me.
