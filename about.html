Saijean French has been passionate about music from an early age. Always surrounded by music and growing up with a grandfather that had his own locally known jazz band fueled her love and interest in playing guitar at the age of nine. Saijean has been playing guitar for many years. Additionally, she has gained experience and music theory knowledge through private lessons and teaching beginners through intermediate guitar lessons. Saijean also enjoys the piano and teaches piano lessons. She a dedicated guitar and piano instructor and is a passionate musician. Saijean's goal is to grow as a musician and share her knowledge, skill, and experience with others. Saijean has experience in styles such as Jazz, Country, Rock, Blues, and Pop and more.
